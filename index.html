Our Heritage

Preserving the Art of Spices

At Rakesh Masala, we believe that masala is not just an ingredient; it's an emotion. Founded in 1985 in a small market in Delhi, we have stuck to our roots.

We source our raw spices directly from farmers, dry them under natural sunlight, and use traditional cool-grinding technology (LTG) to ensure the volatile oils—which give the spice its aroma—are perfectly preserved.
